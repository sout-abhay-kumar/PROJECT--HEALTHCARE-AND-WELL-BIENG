What is Health?

Health refers to the state of complete physical, mental, and social well-being, not merely the absence of disease or infirmity. Good health is essential for a long and productive life, enabling individuals to lead fulfilling lives.

Why Health is Important

Maintaining good health is crucial for leading a happy life, performing daily activities efficiently, and reducing the risk of chronic diseases. Proper health care, regular exercise, and a balanced diet play key roles in maintaining health.

Key Factors for Maintaining Good Health

  • Regular Exercise: Engaging in physical activity improves cardiovascular health, muscle strength, and mental clarity.
  • Proper Nutrition: A balanced diet supports bodily functions and prevents nutrition-related diseases.
  • Sleep: Adequate rest helps in recovery and boosts overall well-being.
  • Stress Management: Managing stress is key to mental health.
