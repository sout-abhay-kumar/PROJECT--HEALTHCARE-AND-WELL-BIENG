Importance of Nutrition

Nutrition refers to the intake of food, considered in relation to the bodyâ€™s dietary needs. Proper nutrition is essential for maintaining good health, growth, and development.

Balanced Diet

A balanced diet provides all the necessary nutrients the body needs, including carbohydrates, proteins, fats, vitamins, and minerals.

Building a Balanced Diet

  • Carbohydrates: Fuel your body with energy-rich foods like rice, wheat, and potatoes.
  • Proteins: Support muscle growth and repair with proteins from sources like meat, beans, and nuts.
  • Fats: Healthy fats from avocados and nuts help with brain function.
  • Vitamins & Minerals: Vegetables, fruits, and dairy products are rich in essential vitamins and minerals.
